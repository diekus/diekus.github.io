About me @diekus

👋 Hola! My name is Diego González. I am a Product Manager and Developer Advocate working for several years on the Web platform 🌐. I am currently involved with efforts around Progressive Web Apps alongside the Microsoft Edge team. I am also a co-editor of the Device Posture API specification, which explores giving the Web capabilities to adapt to cutting edge Dual Screen and Foldable devices 📱. In the past I have been known to be enthusiastic about WebXR and even further back in time I was a big nerd for stereoscopy on the Web. I've worked as a university teacher 👨‍🏫, software consultant 👨‍💻, and researcher 👨‍🔬 along the years.

Here's a little bit more about me. I was born and raised in Costa Rica 🌎, and have lived in several countries 🌍. I have a PhD in Informatics 💻 and a couple of Master degrees. I like to take pictures as a hobby 📸, play a lot of videogames 🎮, love vector art 🖼 and have a weird obssesion with technology gadgets in general 🛰.

I enjoy spreading the word about new open technology and guiding people to discover how it can help them in their tasks. I also enjoy public speaking, and helping shape the future of the Web platform. I am open for speaking opportunities and mentorship.
